Coronavirus In India LIVE Updates: In his meeting with Chief Ministers of Indian states via video conference, Prime Minister Modi said that the lockdown has been effective for India as it helped India reach a better place as compared to other countries of the world, in the face of the pandemic. The meeting not see any firm decision on lockdown extension, but the focus of the meeting remained around the economic front over the ways to boost back the staggering economy. The last video conference had taken place on April 11, when several Chief Ministers had suggested the Prime Minister to extend the initial 21-day lockdown by another two weeks.
