Odisha Favours Lockdown Extension For Another Month


Odisha Health Minister Naba Kishore Das on Monday said that the COVID-19 lockdown should be extended for another month. "There is a need for the extension of lockdown and it should further be extended by one month," said Das after attending the video-conferencing of Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Chief Ministers of all states.


The Health Minister also said that the Central government should come up with specific guidelines for migrant labourers.
