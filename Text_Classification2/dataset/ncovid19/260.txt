 PM Narendra Modi to interact with CMs today 


Prime Minister Narendra Modi will hold a video conference with the Chief Ministers of all states on Monday amid the coronavirus lockdown.
The Prime Minister has held two such interactions earlier with the Chief Ministers over the COVID-19 situation and lockdown imposed to prevent its spread.
Modi had on March 24 announced a 21-day nationwide lockdown as a precautionary measure to contain the spread of COVID-19
