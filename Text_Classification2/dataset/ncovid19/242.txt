Haryana Health Minister Anil Vij has accused the Delhi of leading to a surge in the number of Coronavirus cases in Haryana. He said, '120 Tablighi Jamaat attendees who came to Haryana from Delhi were Coronavirus positive. We have treated them and now many people from Haryana who had been working in Delhi are crossing the borders after getting passes. They have become Corona carriers. 9 cases of Sonipat are from Delhi'...
